About Kitchenette

Excellence in Bakery Education

Your Gateway to Professional Baking

Kitchenette is a premier bakery training institute dedicated to nurturing the next generation of skilled bakers and pastry chefs. Our comprehensive programs combine traditional techniques with modern innovations, ensuring you receive the highest quality education.

With CTEVT certification, our graduates are recognized professionals ready to excel in the competitive baking industry. We provide hands-on training, industry-standard facilities, and mentorship from accomplished professionals.
